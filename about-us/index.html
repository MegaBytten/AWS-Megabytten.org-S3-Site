About MegaBytten

Simplified, Honest Tech Solutions.

Humble Beginnings

MegaBytten was originally created as a pseudonym for our founder, Ethan de Villiers, when he was learning to develop video-game mods in mid 2019. Since then, the simple pseudonym has progressed into an idea, and from an idea into an organisation.

From originally being a sole entrepeneurship focused on java game and software development, MegaBytten now encompasses various areas of expertise, such as AWS Cloud Architecture, Data Science and Analytics, DevOps pipelines, and even Cyber Security. As we continue to grow and develop, we look fondly on our humble beginnings, and are extremely grateful for those that have been there since the beginning.

Honest Pricing

The Tech industry is constantly bursting with new complex technologies, each integrated with incalculable networks of other technologies. We know this makes the tech-learning curve quite steep, and extensive knowledge difficult to maintain. Unlike other businesses, however, we don't use our expertise to take advantage of our customers.

With the recent boom in cloud-technologies, building infrastructure, services and business has -in truth- never been cheaper. We know this, and have the experience required to cost- and time-effectively optimise these technologies. This is why our prices are far below our competitors, meant to provide you with an honest and fair price.
